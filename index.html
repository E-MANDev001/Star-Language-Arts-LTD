About Us – Star Language Arts

At Star Language Arts, we are passionate about helping individuals unlock global opportunities through language proficiency and effective communication. Our academy specializes in preparing students for internationally recognized English and French proficiency examinations, providing expert guidance, structured training, and the confidence needed to achieve outstanding results.

We offer comprehensive preparation programs for major English language tests such as the International English Language Testing System (IELTS) and the Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL), as well as French language proficiency tests including the Test d’Évaluation de Français (TEF) and the Diplôme d’Études en Langue Française (DELF). Our training programs are designed to equip students with the skills required to succeed academically, professionally, and internationally.

Beyond exam preparation, Star Language Arts is a vibrant hub for language learning and cultural exchange. We offer courses in several global and regional languages, including German, Igbo, Hausa, Mandarin Chinese, and more, helping learners broaden their horizons and connect with people across cultures.

Our teaching approach combines experienced instructors, interactive learning methods, and practical language use to ensure that every learner gains real-world communication skills. Whether you are preparing for an international exam, planning to study abroad, advancing your career, or simply learning a new language for personal growth, Star Language Arts provides the support and expertise you need to succeed.

At Star Language Arts, we believe that language is the bridge to global opportunities, and we are committed to helping our students cross that bridge with confidence.

MEET ME

I wear many hats, and they all have language written on them.

As a university administrator, English coach, academic editor, researcher, and content creator, I spend most of my time helping people speak clearly, write purposefully, and understand language better, whether for exams, academic work, public presentations, or everyday use.

I enjoy working with individuals and institutions to:

  1. prepare for IELTS and other English proficiency exams
  2. edit and proofread theses, manuscripts, and professional documents
  3. explore how language works across contexts, cultures, and communities
  4. create engaging, accessible content on my YouTube channel, Language Arts with Star

My approach is grounded in clarity, curiosity, and care not just ticking boxes, but helping people build confidence through language.
